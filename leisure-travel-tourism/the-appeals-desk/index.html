You are the adjudicator at the Leisure Classification Appeals Desk. A junior clerk has already stamped every activity with three tags — but the clerk keeps taking the easy guess and gets a lot of them wrong. Uphold the tags you agree with, overrule the ones you don't, name the deciding factor, and rule on the case.

1
