Bóthar na Mothúchán

Siúil an bóthar, éist le do chara, agus freagair — The Feelings Road: walk the road, hear how each friend feels, and reply "Tá ___ orm".

🛣️

Fáilte chuig Bóthar na Mothúchán!

Tá tú ag siúl síos an bóthar. Casfaidh tú le cairde atá lán le mothúchán. Ag gach stad, fiafróidh cara díot "Cad é atá ort?" Féach ar an radharc chun an mothúchán a oibriú amach, agus freagair leis an cheann ceart: "Tá ___ orm."

You are walking down a road. At each stop a friend appears, full of a feeling, and asks "How are you feeling?" Read the scene to work out the feeling, and reply with the right "Tá ___ orm." Each stop is worth two points — but if you get stuck you can use a Hint to hear the word, and that costs you a point. Get it right and the friend cheers up. You have three hearts, so choose carefully!

  • 🖼️ Inseoidh an pictiúr duit cén mothúchán atá ann — 2 phointe an ceann! · the picture tells you the feeling — 2 points each!
  • 🔊 I bponc? Úsáid Leid chun an focal a chloisteáil — ach caillfidh tú pointe · stuck? use a Hint to hear the word, but you lose a point.
  • 💛 Roghnaigh, ansin brúigh Abair é! · choose, then press "Say it!" to answer.

Is fearr é le fuaim — works best with the sound on. 🔊
