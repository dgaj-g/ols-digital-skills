Bóthar na Mothúchán

Siúil an bóthar, éist le do chara, agus freagair — The Feelings Road: walk the road, hear how each friend feels, and reply "Tá ___ orm".

🛣️

Fáilte chuig Bóthar na Mothúchán!

Tá tú ag siúl síos an bóthar. Ag gach stad, casfaidh cara ort agus iad lán le mothúchán. Fiafróidh siad díot "Cad é atá ort?" Éist le guth an mhúinteora, féach ar an radharc, agus freagair leis an gceann ceart: "Tá ___ orm."

You are walking down a road. At each stop a friend appears, full of a feeling, and asks "How are you feeling?" Listen to your teacher's voice, read the scene, and reply with the right "Tá ___ orm." Get it right and the friend cheers up and you walk on. You have three hearts — choose carefully!

  • 👂 Brúigh an cnaipe athsheinm chun an focal a chloisteáil arís · tap to hear the word again.
  • 🖼️ Inseoidh an pictiúr duit cén mothúchán atá i gceist · the picture shows you which feeling it is.
  • 💛 Roghnaigh, ansin brúigh Abair é! · choose, then press "Say it!" to answer.
