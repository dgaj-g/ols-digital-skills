Dochtúir na bhFuaimeanna

Éist leis an othar agus aimsigh an áit cheart — The Sound Doctor: listen, then tap the right body part to heal your patient.

🩺

Fáilte chuig an gclinic!

Is tusa an dochtúir. Tá othar tinn. Déarfaidh mé focal Gaeilge os ard — éist go cúramach agus tapáil an áit cheart ar an gcorp chun é a leigheas.

You are the doctor. A patient is sick. You will hear an Irish body-part word spoken aloud — listen, then tap that exact spot on the body to heal it. No words appear on screen, so listen well!

  • 👂 Brúigh an steiteascóp chun an focal a chloisteáil arís · tap the stethoscope to hear it again.
  • 🔄 I mBardán a Ceathair, brúigh Cas thart chun an droim a fheiceáil · turn the patient over for the back.
  • 🏆 Leigheas gach ball chun do Theastas Dochtúra a bhaint amach · heal them all to earn your certificate.

Is fearr é le fuaim — works best with the sound on. 🔊
