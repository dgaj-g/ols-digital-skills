# Credits — Ceart nó Mícheart?

## Flags (`assets/flags/*.svg`)
National flag SVGs sourced from **Wikimedia Commons**. National flags are in the
**public domain** (a flag's design is not subject to copyright); the SVG renderings used
here are likewise published as public domain / CC0 on Commons. Each was independently
verified as the correct, current standard flag for its country before use.

## County colours (`assets/audio` counties + in-code colour banners)
The GAA **county colours** are rendered in-code as simple two-colour banners from verified
hex values (no third-party images, no county crests/trademarks). Colours verified against
the Wikipedia "County colours (Gaelic games)" table, county GAA boards and O'Neills kits.

## Audio (`assets/audio/*.m4a`)
Irish pronunciation of every country and county recorded by the **OLS Irish department**
for this activity. Used with permission for OLS Digital Skills.

## Code
Built for **OLS Digital Skills**. Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, no third-party libraries.
