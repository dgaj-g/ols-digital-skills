How it works

  • Questions get harder as you go — and worth more points: easy ones are 5, then 10, 15, and the hardest are worth 20.
  • Each question has four answers and exactly one is right. A right answer earns the points; a wrong answer just scores zero — there is no penalty for guessing, so never leave one blank.
  • You cannot go back to a question once you move on, and the timer keeps running, so trust your thinking and keep moving.
  • When the timer hits zero, or you finish question 20, the challenge ends and your place on your class leaderboard is locked in.
