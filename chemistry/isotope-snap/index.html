Streak 0

How to play

Two cards appear. A Drawing shows the atom's structure; a Code shows its symbol with mass and atomic number. Press SNAP only when the two cards are isotopes — the same element (same protons / atomic number) but a different number of neutrons (different mass number).

If they are not a pair, do nothing — press Not a pair and the next two appear. You have 10 rounds. Be quick: faster correct answers score more.

Question 1 / 3

Find the relative atomic mass

A mass spectrometer separates the isotopes of an element by mass and measures how abundant each one is. Read the data, build the relative atomic mass (Ar) formula by dragging the numbers and operators into place, press =, then identify the mystery element.

Ar = (mass × abundance) added up, all divided by the total abundance.

Build the Ar calculation — drag the tiles into the boxes

Now identify the element from its Ar.

Type the element's symbol — a capital letter, then a small letter if it has one (e.g. Mg).
