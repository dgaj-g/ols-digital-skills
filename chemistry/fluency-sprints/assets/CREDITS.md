# Media credits — Fluency Sprints

## Hazard pictograms (`ghs/`)
The five GHS hazard pictograms (corrosive, caution/exclamation, toxic/skull,
flammable, explosive) are the official **UN GHS** hazard symbols. They are in the
**public domain** and were sourced from Wikimedia Commons:

- corrosive  — GHS-pictogram-acid.svg
- caution    — GHS-pictogram-exclam.svg
- toxic      — GHS-pictogram-skull.svg
- flammable  — GHS-pictogram-flamme.svg
- explosive  — GHS-pictogram-explos.svg
