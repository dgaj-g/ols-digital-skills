How this works

  • Explore & build. Click any branch, the Checks buttons between them, or a principle below. A panel opens where you write your own summary and add real-life examples.
  • Colour your answers. Use the colour dots in each box to colour-code your notes — for example one colour for definitions, another for examples.
  • It saves itself. Your work is saved automatically — just come back to the same link to carry on.
  • Model answer. Stuck, or want to check? Tap Model answer to show a worked example under each box, for reference. It never changes what you’ve written — tap again to hide it.
  • Test yourself. Switch to Test yourself for a quick quiz on the essentials.
