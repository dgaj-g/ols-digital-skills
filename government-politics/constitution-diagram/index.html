Copy this link. Anyone who opens it sees your exact diagram — paste it into Google Classroom or send it to your teacher. Nothing is uploaded; your work travels inside the link itself.

How this works

  • Explore & build. Click any branch, the Checks buttons between them, or a principle below. A panel opens where you write your own summary and add real-life examples.
  • Colour your answers. Use the colour dots in each box to colour-code your notes — for example one colour for definitions, another for examples.
  • It saves itself. Your work is kept in this browser automatically. Use Save file to keep a copy, or Share link to send your diagram to someone.
  • Model answer. Stuck, or want to check? Model answer fills the diagram with a worked example you can edit.
  • Test yourself. Switch to Test yourself for a quick quiz on the essentials.
